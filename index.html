Personalized Interior Design, Built Around Your Space & Budget

In-home or virtual design consultations by Qasem Abdeljaber, combining professional space planning, precise measurements, custom 3D modeling, and curated furniture recommendations tailored to your style.

What’s Included

Every consultation is designed to give you clarity and direction before making major furniture decisions. Services include space measurement or plan review, furniture layout planning, custom 3D modeling of your space, design guidance based on your preferences, and realistic recommendations aligned with your budget.

Additional guidance may include lighting placement, rug sizing, wall art scale, traffic flow optimization, and planning for future purchases.

How It Works

Start by booking an in-person or virtual consultation. During the session, your space will be measured or reviewed, your goals and style discussed, and a functional layout created to guide furniture selection. A 3D model is provided to help visualize the final result.

Pricing

Design consultations are $150 for locations within 30 minutes of Stash Home Furniture and $200 for locations beyond 30 minutes. Sessions include up to one hour of consultation time.

The $150 consultation fee may be applied toward in-store purchases*.

*Purchase must include at least one complete room or total $3,000 or more to qualify

What You’ll Need to Provide

To ensure the consultation is productive, clients are required to provide a budget range, the number and type of rooms being designed, and whether the consultation is in-person or virtual.

For virtual consultations, accurate floor plans or room measurements must be provided in advance.

Transparency & Experience

QCreates is an independent design consultation service operated by Qasem Abdeljaber. While QCreates is not affiliated with or owned by Stash Home Furniture, Qasem is a sales consultant at Stash and works closely with clients seeking professionally guided furniture solutions.

Who This Is For

This service is ideal for homeowners furnishing new spaces, clients who feel overwhelmed by options, buyers seeking cohesive design rather than individual pieces, and anyone who wants furniture that properly fits their space before committing to a purchase.
