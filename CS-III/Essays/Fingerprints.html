Concept

     For my ISP (independent study project), I've created a game with two other people in a pixelated style. It is an RPG with a simple goal. For the player to get from one side of the island to the other. Once reaching the other side of the island, the player has successfully completed the game and can escape off the island on a tiny boat - eventually leading to the player sinking while on the boat.

Motivation

I've always wanted to make a game. This was the perfect oppurtunity to make the game I've always wanted to start on

Code Segments

This is just a small segment of the overall code with a simple function. As the name of the method suggests, it is meant to recieve damage and is told what to do once the damage is recieved. Firstly, it checks if the player is currently immune to taking damage. If so, then it will not run and the player will not recieve any form of damage. However, if the immunity runs out, then the method runs and the damage deducts the amount of health the player currently has. It also "pushes" the player a small distance depending on the amount of damage acted upon the player. Another thing this method does is it displays the amount of damage taken. Lastly, the code checks if the player's health is less than 0. If so, then the method, "Death", is run and that begins the process of the player dying.
